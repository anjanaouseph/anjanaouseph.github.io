Hello!

Hey I am Anjana ! I was born in Muvattupuzha in the southern state of Kerala, India. I was brought up in Kuwait from the age of 5 and studied at United Indian School in Kuwait till 12th grade, after which I moved to India. I did my undergrad in B.Tech Electronics and Communication Engineering from the prestigious National Institute of Technology Calicut (NIT-C) in the year 2020. Currently, I am doing my Masters degree in Computer Science at Arizona State University (ASU), Tempe, USA. I am passionate about Data Science and Web Development. I have two years of experience as a Software Developer. I am also trying to explore the world of Data Science during my free time. My hobbies include travelling, reading books and binge-watching Netflix!
